Safe & free by design

🛡️Databases open read-only — a stray write raises instead of mutating data.
🚦Every generated query is checked to be SELECT-only before it runs.
💸Runs on the free tier of Gemini or Groq — $0 to try.
🔀Falls back to web search instead of guessing when the data can't answer.
